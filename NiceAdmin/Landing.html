The goal of this project is to develop a software or device (wearable) which is user friendly, economical and caters to needs of people with different disabilities ( deaf and mute by birth, deaf , mute , deaf and mute by accident). This could be done by transcription of speech-to-text (using Google Speech API) or speech-to-sign language when a person(who is neither deaf nor mute) wants to communicate with deaf and mute person. When the deaf person wants to communicate with a person the same process can be applied other way around, where transcription can be done from text-to-speech or sign language-to-speech and also by lip syncing-to-speech for people who are deaf and mute by accident. This software would do noise cancellation when communicating in noisy place and also allow the user to choose whom they want to communicate with when there are many people speaking around them.
